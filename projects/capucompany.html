CapuCompany

A VR co-op horror experience inspired by Lethal Company.

About CapuCompany

CapuCompany is a cooperative VR game where players explore abandoned facilities across dangerous moons in search of valuable scrap. Inspired by games like Lethal Company, the game combines horror, exploration and teamwork with VR hand locomotion mechanics.

You and your crew must work together to navigate massive structures, collect resources, and survive the dangers hiding inside.

Game Trailer

Gameplay

  • Explore dangerous moons and abandoned facilities
  • Collect scrap and valuable resources
  • Work together with teammates to survive
  • Navigate environments using VR hand locomotion
  • Face unknown creatures and hazards inside facilities

Game Features

  • Multiplayer cooperative gameplay
  • Online co-op lobbies
  • Room-scale VR support
  • Standing VR gameplay
